\begin{boxe}  \textbf{The Definition of a Derivative:} The derivative of $f(x)$ at $x=a$, is defined as 
    $$f'(a)=\lim_{h\to 0}\frac{f(a+h)-f(a)}{h}$$
    The derivative function of $f(x)$ is defined as
    $$f'(x)=\lim_{h\to 0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}$$
\end{boxe}
